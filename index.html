About

​Geeta University is excited to announce its inaugural Hackathon, a dynamic event designed to foster innovation, creativity, and technical prowess among students. This Hackathon aims to provide a collaborative platform where participants can tackle real-world challenges, develop groundbreaking solutions, and enhance their problem-solving skills.

Building upon our commitment to experiential learning, this event encourages students from diverse disciplines to come together, share ideas, and transform concepts into tangible prototypes. Participants will have the opportunity to work under the guidance of experienced mentors, attend insightful workshops, and network with industry professionals, thereby gaining invaluable exposure to the tech ecosystem.

We invite all aspiring innovators to join us in this exciting journey of discovery and technological advancement.
