Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where you turn your crazy ideas into real projects. We provide hardware, mentors, fun activities, speakers, workshops, food, friends, and so much more. You take care of hacking; weâ€™ll take care of you.

What if I don't have a team?

We will host a team formation event at the beginning of the hackathon so there's no need to hack alone (unless you want to go solo!)

What can I build?

First go through the problem statements and see if you can come up with a solution. Then, think about how you can use tech to solve the problem. You can build anything.

What if I have never coded?

You don't need to know how to code! We have range of workshops during the hackathon that will introduce you to programming languages and developing for hardware!

How is Hackforge25 different?

Hackforge25 is a 24 hour hackathon that is hosted by Geeta University. We are a group of students and mentors who are passionate about technology and want to help you in your journey. We are here to help you learn and grow as a developer.
